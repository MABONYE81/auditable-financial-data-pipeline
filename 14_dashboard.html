— Why this exists

This dashboard reads directly from pipeline.db — a SQLite database built by a repeatable ingestion pipeline that pulls YETI's official financial disclosures from three independent sources (an investor-relations press release, SEC EDGAR's own XBRL exhibit, and a directly-parsed PDF filing), stores every source document with full retrieval metadata, and cross-validates every number that appears in more than one source before marking it trusted. Every figure on this page traces back to a real, cited document — nothing here is estimated or filled in.
